#### A Note on Vibe Checking

>"Vibe checking" is an informal term for cursory unstructured and non-comprehensive evaluation of LLM-powered systems. The idea is to loosely evaluate our system to cover significant and crucial functions where failure would be immediately noticeable and severe.
>
>In essence, it's a first look to ensure your system isn't experiencing catastrophic failure.

3. Prompt: What can I cook with chicken, garlic, butter, honey and soy sauce in fridge?
   - Result: You have some great ingredients on hand! You can make a delicious honey garlic chicken. Here‚Äôs a simple recipe you can follow:

        ### Honey Garlic Chicken

        **Ingredients:**
        - Chicken (breast or thighs)
        - Garlic (minced)
        - Butter
        - Honey
        - Soy sauce
        - Optional: salt, pepper, and any vegetables you may have.

        **Instructions:**

        1. **Prepare the Chicken:**
        - If using chicken breasts, you might want to cut them into smaller pieces or tenderize them for quicker cooking.

        2. **Make the Sauce:**
        - In a bowl, mix together a few tablespoons of soy sauce, a couple of tablespoons of honey, and minced garlic (about 2-4 cloves, depending on your preference). Adjust the honey and soy sauce ratio to taste.

        3. **Cook the Chicken:**
        - Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a tablespoon of butter.
        - Once the butter is melted and sizzling, add the chicken pieces. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
        - Cook the chicken until it is golden brown on one side (about 5-7 minutes), then flip and cook for another few minutes until cooked through.

        4. **Add the Sauce:**
        - Pour the honey garlic sauce over the chicken in the skillet.
        - Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for a few minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and glaze the chicken, stirring occasionally to coat the chicken evenly.

        5. **Serve:**
        - Once the chicken is coated in the sauce and cooked through, remove from heat.
        - You can serve it over rice, noodles, or with steamed vegetables.

        ### Optional Add-ins:
        If you have any vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, or snap peas, you can saut√© them in the same pan before adding the chicken to make it a complete meal.

        Enjoy your flavorful dish! Let me know if you need any more help or tips.
